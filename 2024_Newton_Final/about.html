About

Final Project - Geography 575: Interactive Cartography and Geovisualization

Methodology

This application is primarily built using the D3 Javascript Library. The GIS data layers come from ArcGIS Online and were then exported to a GeoJSON format. The GeoJSON was then converted into topoJSON utilizing https://mapshaper.org/. This allowed the map to minimize load times. The childcare price data was split into two separate CSV files based on the year the data came from. Percentages from 2023 were based on the estimated median cost of childcare and the median household income fields. The 2018 data already had the percentages calculated.
